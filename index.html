What We Do

Tairawhiti Community Law Centre (TCLC) provides free legal advice, assistance, and representation to people in the Tairawhiti region who cannot afford legal services or qualify for legal aid. They also offer free legal information and education to the community. Their services cover areas like money troubles, housing, employment, family matters, and health-related issuesWe support whānau in need with services including:
Family Disputes, Protection Orders, Parenting & Visitation,
Wills, End-of-life Planning, Māori Land & Whānau Trusts.

Who Are We?

The Tairawhiti Community Law Centre delivers free legal services (which includes free legal information and law related education to all, and free legal advice, assistance and limited representation) to those people who meet our financial criteria to the community of Tairawhiti which are accessible and which nurture and maintain the manner and dignity of all persons that access those services
