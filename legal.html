Our Legal Services

Tairāwhiti Community Law offers a wide range of free legal services for eligible individuals and whānau across the Tairāwhiti region. Our focus is on providing support with issues that affect everyday life and family wellbeing. Our team works with manaakitanga and integrity to support your legal needs.

Family Law

  • Family Disputes
  • Parenting and Child Contact Arrangements
  • Protection Orders
  • Care of Children & Custody

Wills & End-of-Life

  • Wills and Estate Planning
  • Enduring Powers of Attorney
  • Support with deceased estates

Māori Land & Whānau Trusts

  • Māori Land Court processes
  • Setting up or managing Whānau Trusts
  • Succession applications

Other Areas

  • Tenancy & Housing issues
  • Employment advice
  • Debt, fines and financial matters
  • Community Education on legal topics
